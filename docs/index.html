1 Owener's Policies

An Owner's Policy insures a purchaser of real property that the title to the property is vested in that purchaser and that the property is free from all defects, liens and encumbrances exept those listed as exceptions in the policy or are excluded from the scope of the policy's coverage. The liability limit if the owner's policy is typically the purchase price paid for the property.
